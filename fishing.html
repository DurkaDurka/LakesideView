Fishing

The lake is well stocked with varied species of Carp and some Catfish, offering good sport from quality fish. Day tickets are £7 for 2 rods per person - dawn till dusk. The lake is also available to book for matches or private use at £50 per day. Fishing rules include: barbless hooks only, ground bait in moderation, no keep nets (except in matches). Popular baits include: maggots, bread, sweetcorn, luncheon meat, cheese & dog biscuits.
