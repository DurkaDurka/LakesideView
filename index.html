About us

Nestled in the south Northamptonshire countryside, Lakeside View is a picturesque caravan and camping site overlooking a delightful lake, which is naturally spring fed and visited by an array of different wildlife. This well established site is open all year round and for the motoring enthusiast, we are ideally located for Silverstone race circuit.
